mka_ical

Description:

This plugin provides a txp-Tag mka_ical which makes it
easy to display dates of an ICS-Calendar-File.

It makes use of a PHP-Class called SG_iCalReader by Morten Fangel.

Examples:

<txp:mka_ical />
